General

?What is MedWHOLE Alliance?
  - MedWHOLE Alliance for Health and Development (MedWHOLE) is a registered non-profit, faith-driven organization dedicated to helping individuals live boundless and fulfilling lives through interventions that promote whole health, whole persons, and whole communities.
 Our comprehensive public health organization operates across Nigeria through three integrated arms: MedWHOLE Consult (technical assistance and advisory services), MedWHOLE Academy (capacity building and training), and MedWHOLE Health (direct health service delivery). We work to strengthen health systems, build capacity, and improve health outcomes across the continent.
?Where does MedWHOLE Alliance operate?
  - We operate primarily in the FCT then across other states in Nigeria. our programs reach communities through partnerships with governments, NGOs, and local organizations.
?How is MedWHOLE Alliance funded?
  - MedWHOLE Alliance is funded through a combination of sources including grants from international donors and foundations, contracts with governments and international organizations, program fees for consult and training services, and partnerships with private sector organizations. We maintain financial transparency and publish annual reports detailing our funding and expenditures.
?How can my organization partner with MedWHOLE Alliance?
  - We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our commitment to improving health outcomes in Nigeria. Partnership opportunities include collaborative program implementation, technical assistance arrangements, training and capacity building partnerships, and research collaborations. Please contact us through our website to discuss potential partnership opportunities.

Training Programs

?Who can enroll in MedWHOLE Academy programs?
  - Our programs are designed for public health professionals, healthcare workers, program managers, researchers, and anyone working in health-related fields. Specific prerequisites vary by program and are listed in each program description. We welcome participants from all Nigerian countries and beyond.
?Are the training programs accredited?
  - Yes, MedWHOLE Academy programs are accredited by relevant professional bodies and educational authorities. Participants receive certificates upon successful completion. Some programs also offer continuing education credits for licensed professionals.
?What is the format of the training programs?
  - We offer programs in three formats: fully online (accessible from anywhere with internet), hybrid (combination of online learning and in-person workshops), and in-person (intensive workshops at our training centers). The format for each program is clearly indicated in the program description.
?How much do training programs cost?
  - Program fees vary depending on the duration, format, and content. Contact us for more details.
?Do you offer scholarships or financial assistance?
  - Yes, we offer partial and full scholarships based on financial need and merit. We also provide payment plans and group discounts for organizations enrolling multiple participants. 

Consulting Services

?What consulting services does MedWHOLE offer?
  - MedWHOLE Consulting the following services:  Facility and Systems Assessment, Public Health Research, Policy Design and Technical Support, Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL), and Training and Advisory. We tailor our services to meet specific client needs.
?How do I request consulting services?
  - To request consulting services, please contact us through our website with details about your organization and the technical assistance you need. Our team will schedule a consultation to discuss your needs, scope of work, timeline, and budget. We then develop a customized proposal for your review.
?What is the typical timeline for consulting projects?
  - Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Short-term technical assistance may be completed in 1-3 months, while comprehensive health systems strengthening projects typically span 1-3 years. We work with clients to develop realistic timelines that meet their needs and constraints.
?Can MedWHOLE help with grant proposal development?
  - Yes, we provide grant proposal development support including concept note development, full proposal writing, budget development, and proposal review and strengthening. We have a strong track record of helping organizations secure funding from major donors.

  - Study Methodology: The randomized controlled trial compared communities with trained and supported CHWs to control communities receiving standard care.
